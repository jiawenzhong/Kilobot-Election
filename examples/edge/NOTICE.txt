This anonymous FTP area is intended for the use of staff and students
associated with the University of Manitoba.

If you wish to leave files here for a member of the University, please
deposit them in the "incoming" directory, and notify the recipient so
he may copy them from there.  Files older than ten days are automatically
removed from the "incoming" directory.

Use of this anonymous FTP area by staff and students of the University
is subject to the University's policy on the use of computer facilities.
